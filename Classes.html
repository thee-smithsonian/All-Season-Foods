EAST AFRICAN COUNTRIES.

THE COUNTRIES OF EAST AFRICA.

These are the Countries that make up East Africa.

UGANDA

1.Uganda is the smallest country in East Africa.

2.Uganda is a landlocked country and all its imports pass through Kenya, Tanzania and South Sudan.

3.Uganda is also known as the "Pearl of Africa" because of its beautiful scenary.

KENYA

1.Kenya is the second biggest country in East Africa.

2.Kenya handles most of Uganda's imports and exports.

3.Kenya's capital city is Nairobi and it is a very beautiful city.

TANZANIA

1.Tanzania is the biggest country in East africa.

2.Tanzania has to major cities that is Dar-el-salaam and Dodoma.

3.Tanzania has the tallest mountain in Africa called Mt.Kilimanjaro.
